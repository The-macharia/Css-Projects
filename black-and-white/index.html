who we are

Black and white foundation is a not for profit foundation registered in Kenya as per the Kenyan Law.

Our Mission is to spur entrepreneurship spirit in young people so they can go ahead and build businesses that will change their community for better. We aim to achieve this by working with young people as early as possible to instill the mentality that, they are capable of creating and running businesses that can outlive them.

The notion that we all have to be employed has been the biggest contributor to high joblessness and lack of creativity among many youths of Africa. It is high time that we see that notion comes to an end.

our mission

We will achieve our vision by providing working step-by-step with creatives from an early age, provide mentorship and guidance, workshops and training, and support with both networking and financial , so no creative has to feel overwhelmed ever again by the idea of following their entrepreneur spirit.

our vision

A world where creatives are free to create and use their creativity to build businesses that outlives them and change their communities.

what we do

To bring about the kinds of changes that will help inspire young people to create businesses that impact their communities, we seek to understand where the biggest challenge lies.

Whether the challenge is lack of skills, lack of resources, wrong notion in regards to entrepreneurship, or lack of mentors, we listen and learn so we can identify pressing problems that get too little attention. Then we consider whether we can make a meaningful difference with our influence, network and our investments..
